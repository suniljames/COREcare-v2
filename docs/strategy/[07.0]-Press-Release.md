# NewCo Launches COREcare for Independent Home Care Agencies — With Published Pricing, Unlimited Seats, and a Pledge to Never Raise It

**The first care operations platform built around the caregiver's phone — priced by client served (not by staff seat), with every AI feature included at every tier, vendor support that can see what your caregiver sees, and a public commitment that the price you sign up at is the price you keep.**

**SAN CARLOS, CA — H2 2026** — NewCo today announced the launch of **COREcare**, a home care software platform built for the way caregivers actually work — and priced for the way agency owners actually run their businesses. About 4 in 5 caregivers leave their agencies every year, and the software they use is built to charge for every one of them, even the ones who have already quit. COREcare is built differently. It charges by the number of clients an agency serves — not by how many people log in. Every staff member, every family member, every AI feature is included. And the price an agency signs up at today is locked, in writing, forever.

**The Problem**

Independent home care agency owners are running real businesses on software built for someone else. Caregiver turnover is the single biggest cost in home care — and the tools agencies use every day make it worse, not better. A 30-client agency that hires 90 caregivers a year (because 80% of them quit) ends up paying for seats that turned over four months ago. Office schedulers lose 15 hours a week to spreadsheets only they understand. The clinical depth for an LTC-insurance audit lives in one tool, state-labor compliance lives in nobody's tool, and renewal time brings price increases nobody warned them about. Meanwhile, the biggest platforms in the category are built for huge franchise networks or enterprise buyers. The independent agency serving 20 to 80 clients gets whatever's left over — at a price quoted behind a sales call, on a contract that locks in for years.

**The Solution**

COREcare is one system that runs the whole agency. Scheduling, visit documentation with real clinical depth, EVV handoff, care plans that drive both the visit and the bill, credential gates on the schedule, payroll that respects state labor law, QuickBooks-ready billing with first-class invoice corrections, expense reimbursement, and a built-in family portal. The caregiver's phone is the primary surface; office staff use a web dashboard. AI handles the heavy lift on visit notes, scheduling, and care planning — included at every tier, never trained on your data. When you need help, you reach an engineer who built the product, who can — with your authorization, fully audited — see exactly what your caregiver is seeing and fix it. Underneath, every action is in an append-only audit log. An audit becomes a report you pull in a few clicks, not a three-week scramble.

**What Makes This Different — Starting With the Bill**

COREcare's pricing page lists three tiers, no sales call required: **Starter at $349/month** for agencies with up to 20 active clients, **Growth at $749/month** for up to 60 clients, and **Scale at $1,499/month** for up to 150 clients. Beyond the included clients, each tier has a published per-client overage rate ($12, $10, and $8 respectively). Staff seats are unlimited. Family portal seats are unlimited. AI is included. Onboarding is self-serve and free. Contracts are month-to-month by default. And two commitments are printed directly on the pricing page: **the price an agency signs up at will never be raised**, and **every January, NewCo publishes a one-page report on what its software cost to run that year, how that changed, and whether the savings went into new features, lower prices, or company margin**. No competitor in this category has made either commitment.
